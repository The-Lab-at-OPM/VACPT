Support for  providers who  support Veterans

Community providers play a vital role in ensuring that America’s Veterans receive the care they have earned.

The Community Provider Toolkit is a resource for health care professionals working with Veterans outside of the VA health care system.

This site offers information and tools relevant to Veterans’ mental health and well-being curated especially for community providers by clinicians at the VA’s National Center for PTSD and Office of Mental Health and Suicide Prevention.
